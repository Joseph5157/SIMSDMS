Getting Setup

Signing In & Navigation Map

Sign in once using a secure passwordless OTP delivered straight to your Telegram account. Navigate using the bottom tabs.

  1. Open the app link on your browser and type in your registered email.

  2. Tap 'Request OTP'. The system will send a secure 6-digit code via Telegram.

  3. Enter the code in the verification panel. No password required.

  4. If you haven't linked Telegram, tap the invite link sent by your administrator to activate.

💡 PRO TIP: Add the app to your phone's Home Screen (Add to Home Screen button in browser) to run it like a native application!

Task 01 · Shift Days

Checking In & Checking Out

This simple task replaces the manual register. It records your exact entry and exit times for disciplinary audit logs.

  1. Open the Dashboard. Today's duty slot will appear at the top in a blue card.

  2. Upon arriving at your duty area, tap the white 'Check In' button.

  3. Before leaving the duty spot at the end of the session, open the app and tap 'Check Out'.

  4. If you have both Morning & Afternoon shifts, check in and out of each separately.

⚠️ DON'T FORGET: Missed checkouts will be auto-clocked out at the session cutoff time. This registers as an "Auto" checkout status and is flagged for review.

Task 02 · Start of the Month

Picking Your Scheduled Duty Slots

At the start of the month, the scheduling window opens. You pick which dates you are available to stand gate duty.

  1. Navigate to the 'Slots' tab to view the active month's calendar.

  2. Dates with available slots will be highlighted with a blue border.

  3. Tap a date, then click 'Pick Morning' or 'Pick Afternoon' in the drawer.

  4. Your chosen dates will turn green. Complete your required target count.

💡 COORDINATION NOTES: Once you reach your required target slots, the window locks. You can request reassignment (Task 4) if you need to alter dates later.

Task 03 · During Duty

Logging Student Infractions

Log student violations in real-time during your shift. The system automatically pulls up the active student registry and predefined fines.

  1. You must be checked in. Tap 'Record Student Violation' on the Dashboard.

  2. Search for the student by typing their name or Registration Number.

  3. Select the Violation Type. The fine amount will auto-fill from the system database.

  4. Tick the 'Warning only' checkbox if a cash penalty is not applicable, add notes, and submit.

🚀 QUICK-ADD MODE: Logging a group? Turn on 'Quick-Add Mode' at the bottom of the drawer to log multiple student infractions in a row without the form resetting.

Task 04 · Schedule Changes

Requesting a Duty Swap (Reassignment)

If you cannot stand an upcoming duty shift, you can broadcast a cover request to an eligible colleague.

  1. On your Dashboard, go to 'Upcoming Duties'.

  2. Find the date and tap the '🔄 Request Reassignment' button.

  3. Choose an eligible colleague from the dropdown list and enter a brief reason.

  4. Tap 'Send Request'. The slot status updates to 'Requested - Pending'.

⚠️ STILL YOUR SHIFT: Sending a swap request does not immediately remove your responsibility. You are still assigned to the shift until your colleague accepts.

Task 05 · Colleague Requests

Answering Inbound Cover Requests

When a colleague requests you to stand in for them, you will receive an alert in Telegram and a pending action on your Dashboard.

  1. Inbound requests are listed at the top of your Dashboard under 'Reassignment Requests'.

  2. Check the date, session type, requesting teacher, and their note.

  3. Tap the green 'Accept' button to take the shift. It immediately syncs to your schedule.

  4. Or tap 'Reject' if you are unavailable. The shift returns to the requester.

⚡ TELEGRAM INTEGRATION: You can click the 'Accept' or 'Decline' buttons directly inside your Telegram notification message without opening a browser.

Self-Audits

Tracking History & Deleting Mistakes

Two tabs allow you to monitor your history and self-correct details before admin locking.

  1. 'Attendance' Tab: Displays monthly summaries of your duty slots—on-time checks, late checks, and auto clock-outs.

  2. 'Violations' Tab: Reviews every infraction you have logged during the semester.

  3. Deletes: If you log a student in error, tap 'Delete' on the violation card to remove it instantly.

  4. Flags: If a student disputes a claim, tap 'Flag for Review' to send it to the administrator drawer.

⚠️ DELETION POLICIES: Deleting a record is permanent. Once an administrator processes the monthly fines register, violations are locked and cannot be deleted.

Help & Notifications

Telegram Notifications & Help Desk

The Telegram bot acts as your active assistant. It keeps you informed of your duty schedule automatically.

  1. Duty Reminders: Triggered 30 minutes before your assigned shift starts.

  2. Clock-Out Alerts: Delivered 15 minutes before the shift cutoff hour to prompt manual checkout.

  3. Bot Commands: Type '/menu' inside the Telegram chat to view schedules, next duty, and open scheduling dates.

🆘 TECHNICAL HELP: If you are locked out (5 failed OTP attempts), if OTPs do not arrive, or if you need to override a duty schedule date—contact your Administrator directly.

SIMS DMS — Faculty Quick Reference

Widescreen Reference

🔑 1. Sign In

  • Enter registered email on app.
  • OTP delivered via Telegram Bot.
  • Input code to log in. No passwords.

📋 2. Check In & Out

  • Dashboard → Today's Duty Card.
  • Tap Check In on arrival.
  • Tap Check Out on departure.

📅 3. Pick Slots

  • Go to 'Slots' tab.
  • Select active blue dates on grid.
  • Choose Pick Morning/Afternoon.

⚠️ 4. Log Infraction

  • Must check in first.
  • Tap Record Student Violation.
  • Search student & choose type (presets).

🔄 5. Swap Duties

  • Dashboard → Upcoming Duties list.
  • Tap 'Request Reassignment'.
  • Pick colleague (remains pending until accepted).

✅ 6. Review & Manage

  • 'Attendance' tab: view check-in stats.
  • 'Violations' tab: view logged records.
  • Tap 'Delete' or 'Flag' to resolve errors.

Important Rules to Keep in Mind

• Only log student violations while on active check-in duty. • Fines are preset; you do not decide the cash penalty. • Missed checkouts register as "Auto" clock-out and are flagged. • Deletes are permanent and lock at the end of the month.

Technical Support Contacts

For OTP issues, lockout resets, or custom overrides, contact your Admin. Type /menu in Telegram to get schedule shortcuts.
